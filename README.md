# Routent

**Navigate smarter. Discover deals along every route.**

Hyper-local navigation and deals discovery platform connecting travelers with local shops and offers in real-time.

**Project Repository:** [https://github.com/tbsvipul/allonbizz.git](https://github.com/tbsvipul/allonbizz.git)

---

## 📱 Platform Overview

Routent consists of **three interconnected portals**:

### 1. Customer Mobile App (iOS & Android)
- Real-time navigation with live offer pins along your route
- Discover deals, filter by interest tags, save favorites
- Track past journeys and more

### 2. Keeper Portal (Web)
- For shop & restaurant owners
- Manage listings, create offers, respond to reviews, broadcast notifications

### 3. Admin Panel (Web)
- Operations team dashboard
- Moderate content, approve keepers, analytics, system management

## ✨ Key Features

### Customer App
- **Live Map Navigation** with offer pins
- **Interest Tag Filtering** (Veg, Coffee, Fashion, etc.)
- **Discover Feed** & personalized recommendations
- **Offer Details & Shop Profiles**
- **Past Journeys History**
- **Notifications** (offer alerts)
- **Profile & Preferences** (location marker, saved items)

### Keeper Portal
- **4-step Onboarding** with document verification
- **Shop Management** (multiple locations, geofencing)
- **Offer Creation & Management**
- **Broadcast Notifications** to nearby users
- **Review Management** & replies
- **Performance Analytics**

### Admin Panel
- **User & Shop Management**
- **Offer Moderation**
- **Journey Analytics** & platform insights
- **Categories & Tags Management**
- **Support Ticket System**
- **Granular Admin Permissions**
